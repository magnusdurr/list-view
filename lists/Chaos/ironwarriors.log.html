Iron Warriors is one of the few Chaos Space Marine armies that lacks daemons and Chaos Sorcerers, and so start out inherently weaker than other lists. The challenge is compensate for this lack without creating builds that are too powerful. Overall, the idea is to enable a very firepower heavy playstyle with plenty of unique units and formations.

Bionics and Battlefield Repairs: The same special rules as the loyalist Iron Hands have been introduced to the list, making certain units and formations a bit tougher.

Obliterators: Iron Warriors has special access to Obliterators. While they cannot be taken as a separate formation, several formations in the army have access to them, in particular the Iron Warriors Retinue and the Tyrant Terminators.

Ordinatus: The Ordinatus Majoris has tweaked stats and is significantly cheaper, as it is severly overpriced in other lists given how fragile the unit is. The list also receives the Ordinatus Minoris in chaos format. The Siegelord and Ravager Titans has been removed from the list due to these inclusions.

Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • Defilers are part of the 33% section, along with the newly introduced Maulerfiends.
  • Predators and Land Raiders can be taken along with Vindicators in the core section.
  • Tyrant Terminators with Cyclone Missile Launchers replaces Chaos Terminators.
  • Siege Rhinos are introduced as an alternative to normal Chaos Rhinos.
  • Havocs are part of the Core formations, but are forced to have Land Raiders. They can also be taken as a cheap upgrade to the Retinues.
  • Two Bezashk formations have been introduced, to represent the Iron Warrior's willingness to use more extreme types of munitions.
  • The Iron Warriors Basilisks have been raised in cost, but now has the option to be taken in a formation of three.
  • Hell Talons and Hell Blades have been replaced with the Heldrake formation, since the list also has the cheaper and slightly more fragile Harbringer Bomber.
