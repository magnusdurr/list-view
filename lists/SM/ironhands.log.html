The list has a strong focus on mechanized infantry, armored vehicles and Dreadnoughts and has access to many unique Dreadnought and Land Raider configurations. The list is designed for a ground based play style but also has some formations that works well when deployed from space. The list also has access to Thunderhawk Transporters as a way to air transport the mechanized infantry or armoured formations.

Since the Iron Hands chapter has strong ties to the Adeptus Mechanicus the list has been given access to more flexible weapons configuration on their Titans. The list has access to a Techmarine character with unique abilities to "repair" armoured vehicles, and the list also has a unique Devastators using powerful (but short ranged) Graviton Guns.

In general, most Iron Hands formations are powerful but expensive. Do not expect to have a high number of activations and do not try to do everything at the same time. Including both space drops, titans and air transports will almost certainly spread your focus too thin...
