Scenarios

The EA:Ragnarök tournament game rules are designed to be used with the EA:Ragnarök army lists. They will allow two players to fight an evenly balanced battle with little or no preparation.

 
Fire the Beacons
Fire the Beacons is a new scenario over three turns. While the rules and victory conditions are familiar from the NetEA standard scenario, in Fire the Beacons you accumulate points from the first turn onwards. Fire the Beacons should be considered the standard scenario for EA:Ragnarök.
 
Battle Line
The Battle Line scenario is based on the scenario described in the NetEA Epic Tournament Game Rules. We have done a few tweaks which we hope will improve the scenario and pose some new challenges in a well known context.
